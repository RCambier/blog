Potatoes

A farmer has 100kg of potatoes. At the start, they are composed of 99% of water (the water is 99% of the total weight) and 1% of dry matter (the dry matter is 1% of the total weight). Later, during storage and because of evaporation, the water percentage drops to 98%. What is the total weight of the potatoes then?
